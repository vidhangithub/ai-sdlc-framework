## 7. Out of Scope

The following are explicitly NOT in scope for this requirement:

- Creating, updating, or deleting bank account records (read-only API)
- Transaction history retrieval
- Account statement generation or PDF export
- Real-time balance refresh from core banking system
- Customer identity management or KYC
- Batch/bulk account data export

> No explicit out-of-scope items were stated in the source — the above are inferred. Recommend PO clarifies during LRS review.
